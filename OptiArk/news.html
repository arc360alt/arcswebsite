OptiArk News & Updates

OptiArk Version 1.4.0 "Rewrite" Released!

May 14, 2025

We're excited to announce the release of OptiArk version 1.4.0! This version includes a significant rewrite of the pack aimed at improving performance and stability. We've also addressed several bugs from previous versions.

Key changes include potential FPS improvements and a warning about entity culling (please keep it disabled for now). Linux users on Wayland should still consider the WayFix mod for the best experience.

Grab the latest version from the downloads page!

Performance Boost with Version 1.3.0

May 14, 2025

Version 1.3.0 brought even more optimizations to OptiArk! We've fine-tuned the mod list, removing some less effective mods and adding new ones that have shown significant performance gains. Users with high-end hardware like the RTX 4090 have reported FPS increases from around 1400 to 1700!

We've also disabled the dynamic crosshair by default for a cleaner experience, but you can re-enable it if you prefer. As always, some mods were updated to their latest versions.

We're thrilled to launch our brand new website for OptiArk! Here you'll find all the information you need about the modpack, including features, performance benchmarks, download links, and now, a dedicated news section to keep you updated on the latest developments.
