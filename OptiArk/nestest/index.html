OptiArk Features

Performant Gameplay

Experience high framerates on even weaker hardware. About 300-1000FPS depending on settings, but we do have some good premade settings that are the bare minimum to make your gameplay the smoothest it can be.

Runs Great Everywhere

OptiArk is created for efficiency and framerate. It runs pretty great on a wide spectrum of hardware (that can run java 21+), delivering high framerates and some qol mods without demanding top-tier specs.
